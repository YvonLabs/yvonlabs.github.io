ToastKit Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 26, 2025

ToastKit is a browser extension built by YvonLabs that helps users quickly clear browser data and reset privacy preferences with one click. We designed ToastKit to operate fully on-device, meaning it does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

ToastKit does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information, browsing history, cookies, or usage data. All extension functions operate locally within your browser.

Permissions

ToastKit requests certain browser permissions (such as browsingData and activeTab) solely to enable its reset functionality. These permissions are used exclusively within your browser and never involve data transfer to external servers.

Third-Party Services

ToastKit does not integrate with, send data to, or rely on any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the extension or applicable regulations. Updates will always be posted on this page.
